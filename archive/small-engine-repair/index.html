Equipment Maintenance Tips

Simple steps to keep your small engines running smoothly and extend their life.

Regular Oil Changes

Change oil at least once per season or every 25 hours of use. Clean oil prevents engine wear and ensures reliable operation.

Air Filter Maintenance

Clean or replace air filters regularly, especially in dusty Arizona conditions. A clean filter improves performance and engine life.

Fuel System Care

Use fresh fuel and add stabilizer if storing equipment. Old fuel causes most starting and performance problems.

Spark Plug Service

Replace spark plugs annually for easy starting and smooth operation. A small investment that prevents bigger problems.

Proper Storage

Store equipment in a dry place and protect from Arizona's intense sun. Proper storage extends equipment life significantly.

Professional Service

Schedule annual tune-ups to catch problems early and keep equipment running at peak performance throughout the season.
