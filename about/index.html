Our Company Timeline

1983

The Beginning

Paul Yzagere Sr. founded Yzagere Enterprises with a simple mission: provide honest, reliable home services to the growing Glendale community. Starting with basic lawn care and handyman services, we quickly built a reputation for quality work and fair pricing.

1995

Service Expansion

Recognizing the unique needs of Arizona homeowners, we expanded our expertise to include specialized sprinkler and irrigation repair. The desert climate demanded water-efficient solutions, and we became the go-to experts for complex irrigation systems across the West Valley.

2010

Full-Service Provider

Adding small engine repair and junk hauling services, we became a comprehensive home service provider. Our commitment to staying current with technology while maintaining old-fashioned service values set us apart in an increasingly competitive market.

TODAY

Third Generation Strong

Now proudly operated by the third generation of the Yzagere family, we've maintained the founding principles of integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction while adapting to serve modern needs. Many of our customers have been with us for decades.
